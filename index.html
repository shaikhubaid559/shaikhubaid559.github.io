6) How to Track Progress (simple & non-sugarcoated)

  • Keep a training log: date, exercise, sets, reps, RPE (1–10), notes on form.
  • If reps stagnate for 3 sessions, reduce load or change variation — not forever; adjust and push again.
  • Measure 4 key metrics every 4 weeks: max strict pull-ups, max dips, one tight pistol, hanging leg raise reps.
