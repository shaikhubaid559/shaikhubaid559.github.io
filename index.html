About

Welcome to Ubaid Calisthenics — a platform for athletes who want to master their bodyweight. This program will take you from beginner to advanced level using strength, skill, and discipline.

Beginner Level

  • Push-ups – 3 sets of 10–15
  • Australian Pull-ups – 3 sets of 8–12
  • Bodyweight Squats – 3 sets of 20
  • Plank – 3 sets of 30–60 sec
  • Dips (assisted if needed) – 3 sets of 8

Intermediate Level

  • Pull-ups – 4 sets of 8–12
  • Dips – 4 sets of 10–12
  • Pike Push-ups – 4 sets of 10
  • Hanging Knee Raises – 3 sets of 15
  • Bulgarian Split Squats – 3 sets of 10 per leg

Advanced Level

  • Muscle-ups – 5 sets of 5
  • Handstand Push-ups – 4 sets of 5–8
  • Front Lever Tuck Holds – 4 sets of 10–15 sec
  • Pistol Squats – 3 sets of 10 per leg
  • Weighted Pull-ups – 4 sets of 6–8

Progressive Overload System

Progress happens when your body is challenged beyond comfort. Follow this rule every 2–3 weeks:

  • +1 rep or +1 set in each exercise
  • Shorten rest time by 10–15 sec
  • Add small weight (2–5 kg) once bodyweight sets feel easy
  • Track every workout to measure real progress
